# ReelNotes

ReelNotes is a modern, cloud-synced web application that transforms traditional studying into an engaging, short-form media experience. Built with a creator's eye, it takes standard text and image notes and turns them into fast-paced, TikTok-style AI "Reels" using kinetic typography and text-to-speech.

## Key Features

* **Real-Time Cloud Sync:** Powered by Supabase, securely log in and access your subjects, text notes, and picture notes from any device.
* **AI Vision Extraction:** Snap a picture of a whiteboard or textbook, and the integrated Gemini AI Vision model will instantly extract the text and save it to your cloud workspace.
* **The "Reel" AI Tutor:** Select your notes and hit "Start Reel." The app uses Gemini to rewrite your notes into a punchy, easy-to-understand tutor script, and plays it back with a dynamic, word-by-word kinetic text overlay over a looping video background.
* **Sleek UI:** A fully custom, responsive dark-mode interface designed for distraction-free studying.

## Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript
* **Backend & Auth:** [Supabase](https://supabase.com/) (PostgreSQL)
* **AI Integration:** Google [Gemini API](https://ai.google.dev/) (Flash & Flash-Lite models)
* **Hosting:** [Vercel](https://vercel.com/) / Netlify

## Roadmap & Future Plans

ReelNotes is currently optimized as a web application, but the vision doesn't stop here. 
* **Native Mobile App:** A fully dedicated mobile application for iOS and Android is currently in the planning phase, bringing the AI Reel experience natively to your pocket.
* **Advanced AI Summaries:** Auto-generating flashcards and quizzes from uploaded notes.
